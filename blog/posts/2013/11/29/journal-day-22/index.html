Fri 29 November 2013
by Michael Jones

Intentions

  • Finish chapter 4 and read chapter 5 of Real World Haskell.

Results

  • Read chapters 4 & 5.

    Ended up trying to use the github package from Hackage which handles the HTTP requests for you but that seems to be tripping over the content of what is returned so needs more investigation to understand what is going on.
