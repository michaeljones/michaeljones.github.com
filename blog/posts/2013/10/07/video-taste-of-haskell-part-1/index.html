Mon 07 October 2013

tags: haskell

An OSCON 2007 talk introducing Haskell to imperative programmers by Simon Peyton Jones.

I like his pacing and attitude and I think there is a reasonable amount to be learnt despite the fact that the slides are not provided on the site or displayed in the video. I am sure they can be tracked down.

I can't tell at the moment how much I understood purely from the quality of the talk and how much from the previous efforts I've made to get to grips with Haskell.
