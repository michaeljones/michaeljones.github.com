Video: Taste of Haskell - Part 2

Mon 07 October 2013
By Michael Jones

In video.

tags: haskell

Second half of the Simon Peyton-Jones' introduction to Haskell. If I was not convinced before, I am now; this is a really excellent talk to give you some background to Haskell and some pleasantly gentle steps towards understanding it.

Provides examples of pure functions and combining them with code that needs to do the IO before going on to discuss the wider Haskell community.

I really love his presentation manner. It is quite disarming.
