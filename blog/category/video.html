Video: Developers, Entrepreneurs and Depression

Thu 12 December 2013
By Michael Jones

In video.

An excellent talk about going through and dealing with mental illness in the form of Type II Bipolar and ADHD. I don't feel that I have had to deal with these issues myself nor am I aware of any close friends going through it but I am guilty of not understanding it well. Greg Baugues opens up the door a little to allow me begin to see and understand what it might be to deal with these conditions.

He has given the talk a number of times as detailed and linked to on his speaking page.
